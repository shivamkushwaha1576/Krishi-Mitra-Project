Key Government Schemes for Farmers

1. Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Under this scheme, all small and marginal farmers receive minimum income support of up to ₹6,000 per year. This amount is deposited directly into their bank accounts in three installments of ₹2,000 each.

Learn More (Link)

2. Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

This scheme provides insurance coverage and financial support to farmers in the event of crop failure due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases.

Learn More (Link)

3. Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme

This scheme aims to provide farmers with timely and easy access to credit (loans) for their farming and other needs.

Learn More (Link)

4. Soil Health Card Scheme

Under this scheme, the government provides farmers with information about the nutrient status of their soil and recommends the correct dosage of fertilizers to improve crop yield.

Learn More (Link)
