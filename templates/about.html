About Krishi Mitra Portal

Welcome to Krishi Mitra, your "Smart Awareness Portal" dedicated to empowering farmers with technology and real-time information.

This project was developed as a solution to bridge the information gap for farmers, providing them with the tools they need to succeed in a modern agricultural landscape.

Our Mission & Features

Our mission is to combine the power of Python (Flask), live data from external APIs, and Artificial Intelligence (Google Gemini) to create a single, reliable platform. We provide:

  • Live Weather Data: Personalized forecasts from OpenWeatherMap.
  • Smart AI Assistant: Instant answers to complex farming questions (powered by Gemini).
  • Community Support: A Q&A forum for farmers to help each other.
  • Market Awareness: Up-to-date information on crop prices.
  • Knowledge Base: Curated information on Government Schemes.
